Simple Speed Dial has been updated to v2.0!


Just a few important changes to note:

  • It's now possible to Import and Export your settings and thumbnails. Look for it in the settings menu.

  • The sorting of dials is now synced with the bookmarks manager. If your sorting is in reverse after the update, you can change it in the settings with the Sort option.
